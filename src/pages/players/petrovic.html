About the Player

The Serbian international goalkeeper agrred a seven-year contract with a club option of a further year. He made his debut in December 2023, coming on late in a game at Everton when Robert Sanchez was forced off through injury. His first start and first clean sheet came a week later with a win at home to Sheffield United. In his third game, Petrovic saved a penalty in shoot-out that decided a Carabao Cup tie against Newcastle in Chelsea's favour.
