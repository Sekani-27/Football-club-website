About The Player

Thiago Silva has been acknowledged as one of the world's most successful and accomplished defenders, winning silverware in Brazil, Italy, France, now England and at international level. As well as being blessed with pace and strength that make him a formidable opponent on the ground and in the air, being affectionately nicknamed 'The Monster' by the Brazillian and French press, he has the composure and technical ability to play the ball out from the centre-back and a wealth of experience at the highest level of football.
